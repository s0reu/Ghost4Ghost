Read before you queue

House Rules

  1. Legal age only

    You must be of legal age to purchase vapor products in your jurisdiction. No exceptions, no appeals. Anyone who appears underage is removed and the account is banned.

  2. Your laws apply

    What's legal where you live is yours to know and follow. Ghost4Ghost doesn't override local law anywhere, ever.

  3. Camera required

    Duels are live, face-on performance. No camera, no duel. Your feed is live only during previews and rounds.

  4. Keep it clean

    No nudity, sexual content, harassment, hate, threats, or illegal activity — on camera, in tags, anywhere. Zero tolerance means instant ban.

  5. Respect the duel

    Ready up on time, run your trick, take the verdict. Rage-quitting mid-round counts as a forfeit.

  6. Report anything

    Report and End Duel controls stay on screen through every live session. Every report gets human review.

  7. Know your limits

    Tricks are performance, not endurance contests. Take breaks. We make no health or safety claims about vaping — ever.

  8. One ghost per person

    No second accounts, vote-rigging, or ban evasion. The board only means something if it's honest.

  9. This isn't an invitation

    Ghost4Ghost exists for adults already in trick culture. We don't promote vaping to non-users or minors — and neither should you.

Plain-language policy

Privacy

What we access

Your camera (and mic, if you allow it) — only during previews and live duels. A visible indicator is on whenever your feed is live. Streams travel peer-to-peer where possible and are not recorded by default.

What we store

Your tag, duel results, rating, votes you cast, reports you file, your block list, and the timestamp of your age attestation. That's the list.

What we don't do

We don't sell personal data, run ads against your camera feed, or keep duel video after a session ends — except a short clip held for review when a duel is reported.

Reports and moderation

Reporting a duel may capture a brief evidence snapshot so a human can review it. Confirmed violations end in bans; false reporting is itself a violation.

Blocking

Blocked ghosts can't match with you, join your rooms, or watch your duels. Blocks are silent and permanent unless you undo them.

Age

Entry requires a legal-age attestation, and stronger age verification can be required at the account level. Suspected underage accounts are removed.
